Our Story

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To democratize financial education by providing accessible, comprehensive, and practical knowledge about stock markets and investment strategies. We aim to bridge the gap between complex financial concepts and everyday investors, empowering them to make informed decisions.

  • Make financial education accessible to all
  • Provide practical, real-world knowledge
  • Support SEBI-registered professionals

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted platform for financial education, recognized for our commitment to quality, transparency, and the success of our learners. We envision a future where every Indian has access to the knowledge needed to build wealth through informed investing.

  • Lead the financial education revolution
  • Build a community of informed investors
  • Contribute to India's financial literacy

Who We Are

SOSM Services Pvt. Ltd. is a technology-driven platform designed to support SEBI-registered Research Analysts (RAs), Investment Advisers (IAs), educational professionals, and other financial service providers.

What We Do
  • Provide digital infrastructure for financial professionals
  • Offer market data integration and analytics tools
  • Create educational content in regional languages
  • Develop AI-based research tools
  • Build broker referral infrastructure
What We Don't Do
  • Provide investment advice directly
  • Manage portfolios or funds
  • Recommend specific stocks or mutual funds
  • Act as a SEBI-registered RA or IA
